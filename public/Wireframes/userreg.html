    Work History

    At least one complete job is required, except salary. For each job:

    • Employer name
    • Job title
    • Start date
    • End date
    • Leaving Salary (optional)
    • Working time (part or full)
    • Employment type (perm, temp or contract)
    • Skills used
    • Description (100 char min)

    Education

    At least one complete education is required, except for details:

    • Institution name
    • Type (leaving cert, bachelor, master, doctorate, certificate, ?)
    • Degree title
    • Start date
    • End date
    • Type of study (full time, part time)
    • Details (optional)

    What I'm Looking For

    Miscellaneous preferences about new job:

    • Desired salary
    • Desired job title
    • Desired start date/ notice period
    • Desired location
    • Desired working time, part time or full time (box to specify hours)
    • Desired working time, permanent, contract or temp (box to specify duration of contract)
    • Search status (actively looking, open to new ideas, or not looking)
