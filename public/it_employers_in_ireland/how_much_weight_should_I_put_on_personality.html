How much weight should I put on personality?

Personality tags can help you decide if someone who has the necessary skills will "fit in" to the company and the role. The importance of personality can vary from role to role, but it's always critical to hire someone that fits the company culture as a whole. Be sure to separate the personal traits needed for the role from the personality of the person doing the job at the moment, and be open to seeing someone who may be slightly different from the most recent employee in the role.
