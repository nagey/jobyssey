What specifics are candidates most interested in?

The more of these questions your ad answers, the more likely you are to get suitable applicants:

  • Salary and benefits
  • Location and possible means of transportation (which bus stop are you near? Is there parking?)
  • Type of work the company does and size
  • Type of work in this job
  • Travel and usual hours of work?
  • Anything unexpected about the company or job?
