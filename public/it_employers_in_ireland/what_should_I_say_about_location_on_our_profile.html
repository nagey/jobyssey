Our office can be tough for some people to reach (or any other less-than-desirable things). What should I say about location on our profile?

Honesty is the best policy. It’s always best to get anything difficult out of the way first, otherwise you’ll use up a lot of time chatting with candidates before they all tell you they don't have a car (or what have you).

If there is anything about your location that is unclear or more difficult than it might appear at first, explain it explicitly on your profile. It's particularly important to make it clear if you are difficult/ impossible to reach either by car or by public transportation. Making it clear how potential employees would get to work reduces applications from unsuitable candidates.
