How do I contact potential candidates through Jobyssey?

When you decide you'd like to contact a candidate, click on the "Invite" button. An invitation email will be sent through the site to the candidate inviting them to check out the job. If the candidate is also interested, you'll receive an email with their contact information so you can speak to them directly to arrange an interview.
