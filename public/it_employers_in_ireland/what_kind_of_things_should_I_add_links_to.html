What kind of things should I add links to?

Ideally, you should link to a wide variety of things to give visitors a rich variety of information about your company. Some things to consider adding:

  • Your company website
  • Recent projects
  • Media coverage of your company
  • Events you're sponsoring
  • Professional blogs or webpages of employees
  • Articles in the company newsletter
  • Published material written by employees, or transcripts of presentations at conferences, etc.
