Is it worth looking at candidates with a low percentage match for my job?

Sometimes. Be aware that new candidates may have not finished building their profiles yet, so there may not be a lot of detail, even if they truly are a good fit for the role. Generally, it's best to start at the best matching results, and work your way through the list until you have several qualified applicants.
