What shouldn't go on our profile?

  • Huge chunks of pre-written information (such as a company profile from the annual report)- just add links so visitors can decide how much they want to read
  • Vague platitudes (such as "great place to work")- everyone wants concrete details, so come up with some specifics before you start putting together your profile
  • Anything that could be discriminatory- no comments about anything that can't legally be used to make hiring decisions (like "young, energetic work environment")
  • Anything that doesn't "fit"- Don't add any information that creates unrealistic expectations. It's important to make sure that applicants understand and like what your company is all about to get a great success rate.
