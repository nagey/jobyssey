How much information should I add?

It's best to say a little bit about a lot of things, rather than going into a huge amount of detail. Keep your answers short, and focus on what makes your company unique. Give specific examples wherever possible- "garage parking spot" is much more attractive than "great benefits".

It's also good to add lots of different sorts of information- specifics about your industry and business are important, but so are benefits, growth plans, and company culture.

Avoid cutting and pasting huge chunks of text from your website or the annual report. Truly engaged candidates will find their way to that information- your profile should just give a snapshot to get them interest.
