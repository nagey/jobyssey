Our company doesn't offer a lot of benefits, but it's a great place to work. How do I explain that to prospective candidates?

At small companies, there are often perks available that some candidate may find more appealing than VHI and pension packages. Anything could distinguish you from the crowd- a desk by the window, flexible hours, company nights out. It's a good idea to poll recent hires about things that they liked when they joined to help identify anything that you can use to stand out from the pack.
