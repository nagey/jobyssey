How can I help people understand our company culture better?

Personality tags for individual jobs will help candidates understand what you’re looking for. Adding photos to your profile is important to put a "face" on visitors' ideas about your company. Lastly, be sure to mention anything that would be unexpected, or that truly sets you apart in the field, whether it's a fast-paced environment with lots of releases (and late nights), or a strong culture of internal promotion (with 60% of hires promoted after 2 years).
