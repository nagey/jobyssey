What best practices lead to a better hire rate?

  1. Have a 48 hour rule- at every step of the process, a decision as to whether to proceed to the next step should be made within 48 hours, with the goal of moving from CV receipt to offer in around 10 days or so.
  2. Have a clear idea of what you're looking for before you start the search; know exactly what you need to say to get the right person interested. If the hiring manager hasn't made it completely clear to you what the position is all about, starting a search will just waste your time and frustrate people you contact.
  3. Everyone in the interview process should both evaluate the applicant and sell the company- making sure that applicants want to work for you shouldn't just be an afterthought tagged on at the end of the interview, it needs to be given equal thought to get the best possible people working for you.
