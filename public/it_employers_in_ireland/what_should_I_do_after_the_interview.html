What should I do after the interview?

The key to successfully hiring top talent is a timely and transparent process. Get back to candidates within 48 hours of the interview to discuss next steps. If you've reached the point of making an offer, have the written offer ready to send out when you speak with them to ensure the process is completed quickly.
