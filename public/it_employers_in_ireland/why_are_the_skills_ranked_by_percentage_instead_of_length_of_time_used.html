Why are the skills ranked by percentage instead of length of time used?

Year of experience with a particular skill can be deceptive. Often, someone with a year of experience using a skill full time to aggressively develop new features and functions will be more knowledgeable than someone who has used it 40% of the time for 6 years to essentially do the same thing over and over again. Also, it makes it hard to express needing someone who is a high-level expert with a relatively new technology (“5+ years experience developing iPhone apps”?) We feel its more accurate to look at levels of knowledge of a particular skill, verifying that against experience on the CV (and later with technical screening during the interview process).
