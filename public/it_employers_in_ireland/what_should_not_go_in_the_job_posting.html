What should not go in the job posting?

  • General statements about the company or about the responsibilities of the role that could describe any job ("Write high quality code")
  • Anything that could be discriminatory- no comments about anything that can't legally be used to make hiring decisions (like "young, energetic work environment")
  • Superfluous information- candidates just want to know a few of the most important things about the job. Adding a ton of other information makes it hard to pick out the most important details
