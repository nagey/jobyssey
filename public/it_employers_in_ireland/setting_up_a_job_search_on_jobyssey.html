How long should the description be, and what should I include?

It's best to keep the description straightforward, and 4-6 sentences long. Potential applicants want to understand what the main purpose or project related to the role is, what the main things they'll be doing are, who else they'll be working with/reporting to, and what makes this job different from a general role of the same title.
