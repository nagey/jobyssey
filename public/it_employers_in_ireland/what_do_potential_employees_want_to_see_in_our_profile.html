What do potential employees want to see in our profile?

  • Your location (and how to get there via car and public transit)
  • Your basic stats- size, type of industry, etc.
  • A sense of company culture- candidates want to know if they will "fit"
  • Benefits (official benefits like VHi and pension, but things like parking, flexible hours, etc. too)
  • An idea of what makes you unique- Why work for you instead of a competitor? Again, it's best to answer with specifics ("only full tuition reimbursement program in Irish game design industry") than generalizations
