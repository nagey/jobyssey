What sort of interview process should I follow?

A good general process for technical roles is:

  1. Screen CVs; have CVs and work samples screened by hiring manager.
  2. Conduct initial phone interviews and book in-office interviews.
  3. Interview with hiring manager and 1-2 colleagues, including technical screening for specific skills.
  4. Make a decision.

    5. It’s tempting to add on other steps, and sometimes (for senior roles, new roles, or positions with several equally good applicants) it is necessary to go to a second or even third round. In most, cases, however, you’ll have a sense after the first in person meeting if the person is a “yes” or “no”. Consider that often "maybe" people are "no, but I'm worried we're not going to find a yes". Dragging the process out can lead to losing “yes” candidates, and you probably won't like the “no” and “maybe” candidates any better.
