What can I do to be ready to fill a position at any time?

  1. Have a good written description of what each job is about. You should know which responsibilities take up most of the person's time in that role, what skills they need, what background or experience they need, and what the selling points of the role are (such as exciting new projects, etc.)
  2. Have a clear process you user to hire new personnel. Be sure it’s clearly spelled out who is responsible for what so that when the time to look for a new staff member comes, everything runs smoothly.
