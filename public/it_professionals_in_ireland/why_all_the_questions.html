Why all the questions?

Unlike other job boards, Jobyssey works by matching you to jobs that suit your skills. We don’t want to waste your time trolling through tons of irrelevant job postings. Since we’re connecting you directly to employers (no recruitment agencies) we need a full CV and lots of information about you to let hiring managers decide if you’re right without having to use someone else to do a ton of screening.
