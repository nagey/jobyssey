What are you using my contact details for?

Unlike other job boards, Jobyssey stores your contact details confidentially. When employers see your profile, all they will see is a code name, with your real name and contact details removed. When you apply for a job, we pass your contact details along to the employer so that can contact you as necessary throughout the process. You don't have to worry about news of your job search reaching your boss, or getting unwelcome phone calls.

Keep in mind, though, your CV can be viewed by all employers- be sure to delete your name and contact info there (including the filename) if you need your search to remain completely private.
