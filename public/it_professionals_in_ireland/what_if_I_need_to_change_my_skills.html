What if I need to change my skills?

You can login to edit any details you need to on your profile. Be aware that employers often track changes to your profile and frequent changes to your skills set may be seen as untrustworthy. To get the best response, be sure to add all of your skills to your profile, and be sure your work history supports your stated skills.
